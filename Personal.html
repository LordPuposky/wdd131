About Me

My name is Yesid Augusto Romero Ruiz. I was born in Cachipay, Cundinamarca, Colombia, and I have been married for 20 years. I have three wonderful children and currently live in Bogotá, Colombia. I am passionate about reading books on personal development and Stoicism, which continually inspire me to grow and maintain resilience in all aspects of my life.
