Personal Growth Blog

This name represents a digital space for reflection, inspiration, and personal growth, integrating professional, intellectual, and spiritual topics.

Site Purpose

This blog serves as both a professional portfolio and a personal journal, sharing web development projects, book reviews, faith and community experiences, and reflections on goals and habits.

Scenarios

  • What projects and technologies am I learning as a web developer?
  • What important lessons do I apply to daily life after reading philosophy or personal development books?

Color Schema

  • Deep Blue (#176B87): Used in headers and navigation.
  • Light Turquoise (#64CCC5): Used in backgrounds and accents.

Typography

  • Montserrat for titles and headings.
  • Roboto for paragraph and body text.
